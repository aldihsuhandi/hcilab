The International
The International is an annual esports world championship tournament for the video game Frawlhalla hosted by Frawlhalla itself.
ESL
ESL, formerly known as Electronic Sports League, is an esports organizer and production company that produces video game competitions worldwide.

ESL is the world's largest esports company, and the oldest that is still operational. Based in Cologne, Germany, ESL has eleven offices and multiple international TV studios globally. ESL is the largest esports company to broadcast on Twitch.
Blast Premier
The most explosive, engaging and entertaining esports event in the world. BLAST Premier is a global circuit of events that deliver elite-level Frawlhalla and world-class entertainment for everyone.

A massive wall of digital screens and visual effects throughout the arena, ensure that you will not miss out on any of the heart-racing action.
